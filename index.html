Laura Marano was one of the original students on Are You Smarter than a 5th Grader? ... Her sister is also an actress. ... Her first single, “Boombox,” has over 60 million views on YouTube. ... She had her own radio talk show on Radio Disney Laura Marano started acting in the theatre when she was five years old. She has done numerous plays at the Agoura Children's Theatre, and continued her acting in many national commercials. She is now starring in the new Disney Channel pilot, Austin & Ally (2011), where she is playing the title character, "Ally Dawson". Disney star who became known for her role as Ally on the show Austin and Ally after appearing in Without a Trace and Back to You. She's also had roles in Bad Hair Day and was cast as the lead in the films A Sort of Homecoming, The Perfect Date and Saving Zoë.
